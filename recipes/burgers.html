Burgers

Description

The cheesebuger is a classic American sandwich-type meal, consisting of a beef based patty, cheese, sause and salad between 2 bread buns

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Cheese
  • Sause
  • Salad
  • Bread buns

Steps

  1. Fry beef patty
  2. Melt cheese onto beef patty
  3. Put beef patty on bottom bun
  4. Add sause
  5. Add salad
  6. Add top bun
