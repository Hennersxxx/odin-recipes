Chicken Stir Fry

Description

The chicken stir fry is a healthy mixture of chicken, vegetables and either rice or noodles, topped with sauce. It is quick and easy to make and can be customised to suite anyone.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Sause
  • Rice or noodles

Steps

  1. Fry cheicken
  2. Boil rice or noodles
  3. Add vegetables to chicken
  4. Add rice or noodles to chicken and vegetables
  5. Add sauce
