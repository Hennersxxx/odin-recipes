English Breakfast

Description

The English breakfast is a traditional cooked breakfast founded in England. Though it's contents can vary, the staples of an English breakfast are eggs, bacon and bread. English breakfasts can vary from a small portion of a fiew items, to a large portion of traditional items such as black pudding, haggis, sausages, mushrooms and tomatos

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • bacon
  • toast
  • sausages
  • mushrooms
  • tomatos

    • Steps

    1. fry eggs
    2. fry bacon
    3. cook sausages
    4. fry tomatos and mushrooms
    5. toast bread
